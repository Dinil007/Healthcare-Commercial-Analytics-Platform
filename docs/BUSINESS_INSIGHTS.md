# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

The Healthcare Commercial Analytics Platform provides actionable insights into pharmaceutical sales, product performance, regional trends, and customer behavior using SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and Machine Learning.

## Key Insights

### Revenue Analysis

* Total revenue can be tracked across products, regions, and sales channels.
* High-performing products contribute a significant share of overall sales.

### Product Performance

* Products can be ranked based on revenue and quantity sold.
* Top-performing products can be prioritized for marketing and inventory planning.

### Regional Performance

* Revenue analysis by region helps identify strong and weak markets.
* Regional trends support territory planning and resource allocation.

### Customer Analysis

* Revenue by customer type enables segmentation of new and existing customers.
* Sales channel analysis highlights the most effective distribution methods.

### Machine Learning Insights

* Linear Regression provides baseline revenue forecasting.
* Random Forest and XGBoost improve prediction accuracy.
* Doctor Churn Prediction identifies doctors who may stop prescribing, enabling proactive engagement strategies.

## Business Value

* Improves commercial decision-making.
* Supports data-driven sales planning.
* Helps identify growth opportunities.
* Enables predictive analytics for future revenue.
* Enhances executive reporting through interactive dashboards.
