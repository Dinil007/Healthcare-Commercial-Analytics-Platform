# Dashboard Walkthrough

## Executive Overview
The dashboard provides a high-level view of healthcare commercial performance, enabling stakeholders to monitor sales, profitability, and customer behavior.

## KPI Cards
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Revenue per Sale

## Revenue Analysis
- Revenue by Product
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue by Sales Channel

## Customer Analysis
- Revenue by Customer Type
- Distribution of New vs Returning Customers

## Time Analysis
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
- Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) Growth
- Revenue Trends

## Product Performance
- Best-selling products
- Highest revenue-generating products

## Regional Insights
- Compare sales performance across regions
- Identify high-performing markets

## Business Value
The dashboard helps management:
- Monitor KPIs in real time
- Identify growth opportunities
- Evaluate regional performance
- Support data-driven decision-making
